Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna is the ultimate comfort food — layers of rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and tender pasta sheets, all topped with golden, bubbling cheese. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, it’s a hearty dish that will have everyone coming back for seconds.

Ingredients

  • 500g lasagna dough
  • 2 packages of cream
  • 500g mince
  • 500g ham
  • 500g cheese
  • 2 glasses of milk

Steps

  1. Mix the ingredients.
  2. Put it in the oven.
  3. Enjoy the lasagna.
