Cheesecake

Description

This classic cheesecake is rich, creamy, and irresistibly smooth, with a buttery graham cracker crust and a luscious vanilla filling. Perfect on its own or topped with fresh berries, itâ€™s a dessert that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • Heavy cream
  • Cream cheese
  • Biscuits
  • Eggs
  • Sour cream
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cornflour

Steps

  1. Melt the butter and mix with powdered biscuit, press into springform pan.
  2. Mix the remaining ingredients to form the cheesecake cream and pour onto the pan
  3. Bake for a while and let it set on the counter.
